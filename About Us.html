Our Story

Due to the pandemic, the demand for video streaming services is ever-increasing and there are currently a lot of movie streaming platform in the market. Movie Hub is new to the industry but would like to join the competition.

We are a group on aspiring web developers who wants to showcase the skills we acquired from the bootcamp by creating websites that can match giant players like Netflix, Hotstar, YouTube, and others in the market who pioneered the video streaming business model.

As of now, Most of our content are videos on demand but we're planning to produce our own productions in the future.
